Equipment Management

This equipment management web application provides access to and recording of information on equipment inventory and related actions. It is based on the Sensors Extension of Version 2 of the Observations Data Model 2 (ODM2) This interface is designed for monitoring networks using in situ sensors to manage physical infrastructure and link it to observational data.
